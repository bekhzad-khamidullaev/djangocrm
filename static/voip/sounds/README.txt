Place audio tones here for SIPml client:
- ringtone.wav: played on incoming calls (loop)
- ringbacktone.wav: played while calling out (loop)
- dtmf.wav: short tone for keypad presses

Recommendations:
- PCM WAV, 16-bit, 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz (browser-friendly)
- Keep dtmf short (<200ms)
- Keep ringtone/ringback within ~5-10 seconds and seamless looping
